\section{Sensing Nodes}

\subsection{Energy Node}

Energy consumed can be calculated by summing the power over the 60 seconds:

\begin{equation}
    \text{Energy} = \sum_{i=1}^{120} (I_i \times V_i)
\end{equation}

This gives the total energy used in that minute (in watt-seconds or joules).

\subsection{Water Flow Node}

Read water flow every second for 120 seconds, sum the values and upload them.

\begin{align}
    \text{Volume} &{}= \sum_{i=1}^{120} Q_i \times \underbrace{\Delta t}_{=1\text{s}}\\
                  &{}= \sum_{i=1}^{120} Q_i
\end{align}

\subsection{Temperature Node}

Calculate the average temperature over 120 seconds, then upload it.

\begin{equation}
    \text{Average Temperature} = \frac{1}{120} \sum_{i=1}^{120} T_i
\end{equation}
