\chapter{Overview}

\subsection{Problem Definition (Presentation of the Problem)}

Currently there are no solutions that can calculate the amount of resources (water and electricity) used by Airbnb guests. There are monitoring systems that can provide this data, but even if 
the data is collected, there is no way to compare it with average consumption of resources from other guests. 

This is due to several reasons. First, monitoring systems were created to be used 
in a wide variety of applications, not just in the hospitality industry. Second, most of the time these systems are not interoperable with other solutions. And finally, even if they could work
with other systems, there are no applications created to provide useful information on resource usage to guests, hosts and Airbnb.

It is important to mention that there has not been and initiative from Airbnb or any other company to create something like this even if there is interest from the general public to reduce their 
carbon footprint when traveling. The few examples found of people trying to accomplish this were by using their utility bills and doing the calculations manually. This is not only time 
consuming, but also not completely accurate.
